Welcome to Kids Magic Life!

You may be here because a friend told you about us or you may have chanced upon this page, either way be prepared to go on a magical journey with us to explore life’s hidden magic through music, storytelling, dance, theatre, singing and creative learning!

Kids Magic Life has a long history of creating happy, positive, fun, inspiring books, programs and music for children. All our learning programs have been designed for children to understand the art of creating through imagination that is linked to early years learning and development outcomes based on the school framework.

These stories teach children to have more confidence, improve their communication skills and express themselves more creatively leading to a general improved self–esteem and well-being. Children who are taught to create through these vehicles of creativity learn to combine both left andright brain activity which benefit them in so many ways to contribute to the development of the child in a complete and holistic way.

Kids Magic Life says everything let’s create, have fun and make magic happen. We invite you to come along with us on this magical journey!

We live by the saying, “Dream of what you enjoy and then use your imagination to get what you want and feel good”.
